Welcome to the official page of the Bioinformatics Symposium 2026.

The symposium is open to everyone and is a great opportunity to learn about ongoing bioinformatics research across the Einstein community. The day will feature a keynote by [Dr. Olivier Elemento](https://physiology.med.cornell.edu/people/olivier-elemento-ph-d/) from Weill Cornell, faculty flash talks, trainee flash talks, a poster session, prizes for the poster and talk competitions, and food throughout the day.
